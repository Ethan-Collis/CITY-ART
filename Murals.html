The Twin Cities are full of murals around every corner. Representing the diverse communities of the cities.

Lyndale Mural

Greta Mclain

Features students of the Lyndale community.

The Prince Mural

Bob Dylan Mural

Eduardo Kobra

George Floyd Mural

Cadex Herrera, Xena Goldman, Greata Mclain

Features a portrait of George Floyd in front of a vibrant sunflower to potray him, a person of color, in a positive way, which a lot of times gets negative press. The colors and emphasis on the names creates a powerful lasting impact after viewing the mural.

The Phillips Neighborhood Gateway Mural

Greta Mclain

Features a mixture of mosaics and paintings creating a vibrant representation of the Phillips Neighborhood.
